The Office of Ven Gist (OoVG) is a multi-disciplinary design and technology studio producing work balanced by concept, identity, & utility.

The Office

Philosophy

Our goal, every time, is to deliver the absolute best outcome within a Client's budget and timeline. Our process is kept minimal and flexible enough to adjust accordingly to ensure all requirements are met and then some.

We really (really) like to design for good. Significant discounts and credits are provided for Clients delivering social or environmental benefits.

We want to always be there for you for emergent requests. No nickel and diming.

Process

Design Thinking is a powerful and crucial process. It can benefit any mission and help find solutions to most any problem. As such, it is our first stop along the design journey, and constantly revisited throughout all projects. Call it our base camp. From here, we can scale any mountain (or hill).

Sustainability

As Designers, we are responsible for the materials and practices we suggest to our Clients. As such, the Office proudly adheres to the sustainable methodology outlined by The Designers Accord.

Team

Founder
Ven Gist

Ven Gist is a Designer by trade, as well as by nature. He started in Graphic Design, drinking a ton of coffee and hand drawing typography all day. As the bulk of our interactions moved more towards digital, he learned code to facilitate more effective collaboration with developers, leading to better project outcomes. Today, he creates within a broad spectrum of Design & Technology, occasionally pushing beyond.

When out of office, he can most often be found rambling in the woods with his adventure pup, Mesrine.
